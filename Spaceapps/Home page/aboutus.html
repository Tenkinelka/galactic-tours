About Us

We are a travel agent at a space tourism office. Our website not only develop customized itineraries for travellers who want to visit the main bodies in our solar system, but also educate present-day users about the many different objects in our solar system. Our website provides information to space travellers and astronomy enthusiasts to learn more about our solar system.

How it started

Galactic Tours was founded by Cosmo rangers in 2023 in order to provide tourist information to space travellers with information about interesting tourist destinations in space.
